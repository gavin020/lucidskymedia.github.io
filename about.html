About Us

Lucid Sky Media was founded by a creative visionary with over a decade of experience at leading agencies like MediaMonks, crafting award-winning campaigns for brands like Heineken, Adidas, and McDonald’s. Our journey began with a passion for turning ideas into impactful visuals, and today, we bring that expertise to brands and franchises worldwide.
